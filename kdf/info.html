Valorant Esports: Teams and Players

Valorant esports showcases top teams and skilled players from around the world, combining strategy, teamwork, and precise mechanics in competitive gameplay.

Top Teams

Sentinels

Sentinels is one of the most iconic teams in Valorant history. They became famous during the early stage of the game, especially after winning VCT 2021 Masters Reykjavik without losing a single match. The team is known for strong individual skill and star players, especially TenZ. Their playstyle focuses on confidence, fast reactions, and mechanical dominance. Even today, they remain one of the most popular teams with a huge fan base.

Fnatic

Fnatic is a European team famous for their strategy and teamwork. They are considered one of the most consistent teams in Valorant esports. They won VCT 2023 LOCK//IN and VCT 2023 Masters Tokyo, showing their dominance. Fnatic’s strength comes from well-planned tactics, strong communication, and disciplined gameplay rather than just individual skill.

Paper Rex

Paper Rex (PRX) is a team from Southeast Asia known for their unique and aggressive playstyle. They often use unexpected strategies and take risks, which makes their games exciting to watch. Their style is sometimes called “W gaming” (playing bold and fearless). They reached the finals of VCT 2023 Masters Tokyo, proving they can compete at the highest level.

LOUD

LOUD is a top team from Brazil and one of the strongest teams in the world. They won VCT 2022 Champions, becoming world champions. They are known for strong team coordination, smart decision-making, and consistent performance. LOUD represents the rise of Brazilian esports on the global stage.

Famous Players

Tyson Ngo (TenZ)

TenZ is one of the most famous Valorant players in the world. He is known for his incredible aim and fast reflexes. He became a superstar after joining Sentinels and winning Masters Reykjavik 2021. Many players see him as a symbol of pure mechanical skill in Valorant.

Nikita Sirmitev (Derke)

Derke is a top duelist player for Fnatic. He is known for his consistency and accuracy. Unlike flashy players, Derke plays very smart and controlled, making him reliable in important matches. He is one of the best entry fraggers in the game.

Erick Santos (aspas)

aspas is a key player from LOUD and one of the best duelists in the world. He is known for staying calm under pressure and performing well in high-stakes matches. His aim and positioning make him very difficult to beat.

Wang Jing Jie (Jinggg)

Jinggg from Paper Rex is famous for his aggressive and fearless playstyle. He often pushes enemies quickly and creates chaos in matches. His energy and speed make him one of the most exciting players to watch.

Zheng Yongkang (Zmjjkk)

ZmjjKK, also known as KangKang, is one of the best players from China. He is famous for his Operator (sniper) skills and fast reaction time. He became globally recognized during international tournaments, showing that Chinese players can compete at the highest level.
